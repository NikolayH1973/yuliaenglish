Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to everything you're wondering about

How long does it take to start speaking?

Students usually notice a real improvement in pronunciation and speaking confidence within the first month. After 3–6 months of regular lessons, people make substantial progress and can hold confident conversations on topics that interest them.

Are lessons online or in person?

Both! You can choose in-person lessons in Haifa or online lessons via Zoom. Many students combine both formats depending on their schedule.

What's the minimum level to get started?

None! I work with all levels, from complete beginner (A0) to advanced (C2). In the first lesson, we'll do an assessment and build a plan tailored exactly to your level.

Is there a trial lesson?

Yes, we can do one lesson to assess your level. I'll identify any gaps in your knowledge and put together a personal development plan.

What if I start but want to cancel?

You can stop at any time. Payment for lessons already completed isn't refundable, but I'll fully refund the remaining ones.

Do you prepare students for international exams?

Yes! I prepare students for TOEFL, IELTS, and Cambridge exams, as well as the Israeli Bagrut. Every prep course is built specifically around the exam format.
